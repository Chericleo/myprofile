Meet Okwor Deborah Chinaeme

Okwor Deborah Chinaeme, widely known as Cheric Leo or Little Me, is a dynamic and accomplished community leader and blockchain enthusiast. Her mission is to empower the next generation of innovators and thought leaders in the blockchain ecosystem.

Who is Deborah?

  • Co-Lead and Community Manager at Unizik Blockchain Lab (UBL): Inspires and mentors students to explore the vast potential of blockchain technology.
  • Head of Operations at Blockchain Revolution Conference (BRC): Drives education, networking, and innovation in the blockchain space.
  • Tech Club Lead at Borderless Tech Club UNIZIK (BTC UNIZIK): Fosters technical growth and collaboration among students passionate about emerging technologies.

Technical Expertise

In addition to her community leadership, Deborah is a skilled Solidity developer, bringing a strong technical edge to her initiatives. Her ability to combine technical expertise with effective community building allows her to bridge the gap between innovation and real-world applications.
